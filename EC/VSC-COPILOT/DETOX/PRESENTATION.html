Detox Presentation
Key Ideas for Reducing Technology Overuse
By Coronel Maldonado Enrique Ismael and Gerardo Antonio Rodriguez Cabrera
How can you help stop tech overuse at school?
At School
  • Essential tool
  • Understand subjects & hobbies
  • Set limits
  • Study only (no social media)
  • Activities without tech
  • Face-to-face talk
  • Smart use
What can you do to use technology better?
Personal Actions
  • Learning with AI
  • No dependence
  • Study model
  • Ask questions
  • Practice with/without AI
  • Tech = tool, not need
